Fun Facts

Flamingos are not pink. They are born grey, their diet of brine shrimp and blue green algae contains a natural pink dye called canthaxanthin that makes their feathers pink.

Hummingbirds are the only known birds that can also fly backwards. They often do this when retreating away from flowers. They are also the smallest bird in the world and heavy as a sheet of paper.

The most deadly animal in the world isn't a shark, tiger or bear. But something supprisely smaller-the mosquito. According to the World Health Organization, 725,000 of people are killed each year from mosquito-borne diseases.

The shortest living animal in the world is the Mayfly. Its entire adult lifespan is just 24hrs! Mayflies start their life underwater and some of them can only survive for just 5 minutes, the lucky ones might manage to survive a few days.

Koalas can sleep for about 18 to 20 hours a day. Koala need more sleep than most animals, because their diet of eucalyptus leaves contain toxins, are very low in nutrition and barely have any sugar, so they take a large amount time to resting and conserving their energy.
